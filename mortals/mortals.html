The Mortal World

The mortal world is filled with wonders both mortal and mundane. Nobles compete with viziers over worldly influence. Humble fishing towns receive offers from creatures of the deep. Battle-hardened warriors ride drakes into battle. An empire establishes interplanar trade of cotton, salt, and mana. The world spins as ours does - but with a bit more magic helping it turn.
